Features

Calorie counter

The calorie counter web app can also provide additional nutritional information such as the amount of protein, fat, and carbohydrates in each food item, as well as other important vitamins and minerals.
Users can set personalized goals for their caloric intake and track their progress over time.

BMI calculator

To use a BMI calculator web app, the user inputs their height and weight, and the app calculates their BMI. The app then provides an interpretation of the BMI result, which may indicate whether the user is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese, based on established BMI categories.

Nutritional advice

Nutrition and diet advice: We can provide information on healthy eating habits and offer nutritional advice,as well as promote dietary supplements and meal plans.
We Educate members on the importance of macronutrients,such as carbohydrates, proteins, and fats.
